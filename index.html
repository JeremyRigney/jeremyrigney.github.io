01 — What I do

From terabyte-scale telescope data to production models.

I'm a Senior Data Scientist at Optum, with a PhD in astrophysics from Queen's University Belfast. My research centred on observing the Sun and other stars to better understand stellar dynamics — work that meant designing and running large-scale analysis pipelines end to end.

I've led collaborative projects spanning multiple continents, worked in Agile software teams, and published peer-reviewed research. I'm also an experienced science communicator who has represented Ireland and won awards for public engagement.
